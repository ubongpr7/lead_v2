Why You Need LeadEditor.io?

LeadEditor.io helps marketers enhance VSL performance by easily split testing new leads. Upload your VSL, replace or remove sentences, and merge new content seamlessly. Keep voice consistency with Eleven Labs' voice cloning integration.

Effortless Lead Replacement

Delete and replace any part of your VSL, most commonly the lead, and insert new, high-converting sentences.

Voice Cloning for Seamless Consistency

Using Eleven Labs API, you can clone the original voice in your VSL (with permission) and ensure that your new lead sounds identical to the rest of the video.

Add New Leads with Ease

No need to delete anything! Simply add new sentences or a brand-new lead to the start of your VSL and let LeadEditor.io merge it with your existing content.

Assign Custom Video Clips

Add video clips for each new sentence, and our software will seamlessly merge them into your existing video for a professional finish.

Split Test in Seconds

Test different leads to find out which performs best - all in-house, without waiting on external editors.
