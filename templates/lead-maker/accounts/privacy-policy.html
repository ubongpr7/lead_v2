Privacy Policy for LeadEditor.io

1. Introduction

LeadEditor.io is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection practices and your rights regarding your information.

2. Data Collection

Account Information: We collect information provided during account creation, such as name, email, and payment details.

Usage Data: We gather data on interactions with the platform, including IP addresses, cookies, and device information, to enhance the user experience.

Payment Information: Payments are securely processed through third-party providers, and payment details are not stored on our servers.

3. Data Storage and Processing

Data is securely stored on cloud servers, and processing is handled according to industry standards. Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only.

4. Use of Collected Data

Service Enhancement: To personalize your experience and improve platform functionality.

Customer Support: To assist with inquiries and provide technical support.

Marketing: We may send occasional promotional content, which you can opt out of at any time by contacting support@LeadEditor.io.

5. User Rights

Access: Users have the right to request access to their data.

Modification: You may update or correct your information.

Deletion: Users can request data deletion, subject to legal retention requirements.

Contact support for any of these requests.

6. Cookie Policy

Cookies are used to improve functionality. You may control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Security Measures

LeadEditor.io uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your information.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy, and significant changes will be communicated to users.

Contact Information

For questions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact support@LeadEditor.io.
